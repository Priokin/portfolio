TensorNEAT

GitHub: github.com/EMI-Group/tensorneat (91 stars)

  • - Implemented the NEAT algorithm using the JAX framework for tensorized representation and parallel computation.
  • - Achieved 10-100x speedup compared to Python-NEAT.

I was responsible for simplifying the design of the NEAT algorithm, conducting comprehensive experimental research, and testing its performance against other algorithms. I also fine-tuned the parameters to enhance the overall efficiency of the algorithm.

PPO Real-Time Obstacle Avoidance on CARLA Platform

In this project, I implemented PPO for real-time obstacle avoidance on the CARLA simulation platform, a popular platform for autonomous driving research. The goal was to train an agent to navigate a complex environment while avoiding obstacles in real time.

Below is a video showing the PPO agent navigating the CARLA platform, avoiding obstacles, and successfully reaching its navigation point.
